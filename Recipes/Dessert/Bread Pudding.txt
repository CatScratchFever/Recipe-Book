Ingredients:
6 slices day-old bread
2 tbsp melted butter
4 beaten eggs
2 cups milk
1/3 cup of white sugar
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp vanilla extract
pinch of salt

Recipe:

In a small saucepan over low heat, warm milk, butter, vanilla, sugar and salt. Continue cooking just until butter melts. Whisk in eggs.

Add bread cubes to milk-egg mixture and cool in the fridge for 45 min. to an hour.

Afterwards, pre-heat oven to 350 degrees.

Butter a 4-to-6-cup baking dish and fill with the mixture(top with sprinkles of sugar for the crisp bites)

Cook for 30 to 45 min. until custard has set but still wobbly and the edges have browned.
