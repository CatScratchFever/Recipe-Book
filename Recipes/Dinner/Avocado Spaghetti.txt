Ingredients:
1 serving of spaghetti per person
1 avocado per person +1
Lemon juice
1 clove of garlic
1/4 cup olive oil
1/2 handful of fresh basil
(1/2 tbsp of pesto for substitution)
Salt
Parmesan

Recipe:

Cook pasta in boiling salted water.(20 min.)

Scoop out the flesh of your avocados, discarding the skins & pips. 

Pop it into a blender with your lemon juice, garlic, olive oil, salt & basil. 

Blend until smooth & creamy. 

Add more juice, garlic, olive oil, salt, or basil to taste.

Drain pasta and mix together noodles and sauce.

Serve with grated parmesan on top.

Optional! Add grilled chicken to mix.
