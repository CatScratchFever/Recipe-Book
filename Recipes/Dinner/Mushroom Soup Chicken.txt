Ingredients:
4 chicken legs
1 can cream of mushroom
butter
salt and pepper

Recipe:

Preheat oven to 375 degrees.

In an 8x8 pan, butter all sides and place in chicken legs.

Generously salt and pepper chicken legs and rub in the seasonings.

Cover with aluminum foil and place in oven for 30 min.

Remove pan, remove foil, cover chicken in cream of mushroom soup, replace foil.

Place back into oven for 30 min.

Remove and serve with stuffing!
