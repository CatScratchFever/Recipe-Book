Ingredients:
1 Potato per person +1
Salt and Pepper
3 tbsp Butter

Recipe:
Peel and chunk potatos and place into a sauce pan and cover with water.

Set heat to high until boiling, then lower to medium heat.

Cook for 20~ min. or until potatos are fork-tender.

Drain water, add butter to potatos, add salt and pepper.

Mix gently, serve.
