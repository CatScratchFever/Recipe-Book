Ingredients:
1 Cup Water
1/2 Cup Rice
Oil

Recipe:
Fill a saucepan with water.

Add seasonings (salt, pepper, and a spoon of oil) to water.

Heat water on high heat while covered until boiling.

Add rice, cover saucepan and set heat to low.

Cook for 10-15 minutes or until liquid has evaporated entirely.
-If water begins to start to boil and bubble, remove rice from heat until it settles,
but NEVER remove cover.

Afterwards, remove rice from heat and let it sit for 5 min.

Use a rice paddle to fluff the rice and serve.
