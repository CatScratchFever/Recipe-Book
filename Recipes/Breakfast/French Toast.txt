Ingredients:
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg (optional)
1 egg
1/4 cup milk
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
Any bread.


Recipe:
Beat egg, vanilla, cinnamon, (nutmeg), in a shallow dish. Stir in milk.

Dip bread in egg mixture, coating both sides.

Cook on medium heat until golden brown.

Serve with syrup and powdered sugar!
