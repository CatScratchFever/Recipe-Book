Ingredients:
2 slices of bread per serving
2 eggs per serving
Butter
Salt and pepper

Recipe:
Preheat oven to 375F. Lightly butter both sides of the bread.

Place bread slices onto a cooking sheet and cut out a hole in the center of them.
(place bread holes on the sheet for little crunchy bread circles)

Crack an egg into the holes of the bread, salt and pepper them and place into oven.

Cook for 6-8 min, this will depend on how well done you want the eggs.

Remove from oven and serve.
