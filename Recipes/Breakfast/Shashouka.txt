Ingredients:
1/4 Onion
Garlic Cube/Garlic Glove
Seasoning to taste(Salt, pepper)
2 eggs per serving
1 Can of Tomato Sauce

Recipes:
Dice onion, and satee onions on medium heat in a frying pan for 4-5min(until soft)

Add garlic cube and stir until it melts completely, and incorporate it into the onions.

Add tomato sauce and heat until it begins to bubble.

Crack eggs into tomato sauce, and season to taste, cover pan.

Simmer eggs and sauce for atleast 8 min. or until eggs are firm.

Optional!

Serve on toast!
