Large Batch:

Ingredients:

2 cups Flour
1/4 teaspoon Salt
4 teaspoons of Baking Powder
2 tablespoons Sugar
2 Eggs
1 1/2 cup warm milk
1/2 cup oil
1 teaspoon vanilla


Recipe:

In one mixing bowl, combine together the flour, salt, baking powder, and sugar.

In another bowl, mix together the eggs, warm milk, oil, and vanilla.

Slowly pour the wet mixture into the dry mixture while mixing to avoid clumps.

After all is incorporated, heat up your waffle iron and coat with cooking oil.

Pour in waffle mix into the iron and cook until firm.

Optional!:

Add blueberrys, chopped bananas, strawberries, chocolate chips to add more flavour!
