Ingredients:
2 Eggs
Milk
Some Ham
Some mushrooms
1/4th Yellow Onion
Salt and Pepper


Recipe:

Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Diced onion, ham, and mushrooms. Lightly 2 eggs with a dash of milk, salt and pepper.
Oil a cast iron skillet and bring to heat on medium.
Pour in egg mixture, and add toppings as desired.
Heat the mixture until edges peel away from the skillet.
Place cast iron into the oven for 15 min.
Cool for 5 min, slice and serve.
